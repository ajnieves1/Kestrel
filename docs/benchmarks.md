# Kestrel benchmarks

Measured numbers from real mission runs, generated by `scripts/score_mission.py`.
Detection rate is defects found over ground truth marker count, localization
error is the distance from each detected defect to its nearest ground truth
marker, photos versus frames streamed shows how much of the raw camera feed
makes it from a mission into the report.
